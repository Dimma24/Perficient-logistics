Perficient Logistics Limited

We are a leading transportation and logistics company, founded in June 2022 by Nwadinobi Emmanuel Uchenna. Our team is dedicated to providing top-notch services to our clients, including personal chauffeurs, escort and protocol services, airport shuttle services, inter-state travel, VIP trips and tours, and luxury car rentals with both drive and self-driving options. At Perficient Logistics Limited Limited, we understand the importance of reliable and efficient transportation for both personal and business needs. That's why we strive to provide our clients with the highest level of service, comfort, and safety. Our team of experienced professionals is dedicated to ensuring that every trip with us is a seamless and enjoyable experience.

In addition to our core transportation services, we also offer a range of specialized services for high-level executives, dignitaries, and other VIP clients. Our escort and protocol services ensure that your travels are smooth and hassle-free, while our luxury car rentals provide a touch of class and sophistication to any outing. Thank you for considering Perficient Logistics Limited Limited for all your transportation needs. We look forward to serving you and exceeding your expectations.
